    Meet the Founder

    "Hi, I’m the founder of Berry’s Delight. A creative entrepreneur with one simple goal: to satisfy your cravings with the crunchiest, most delightful popcorn you’ve ever tasted.

    What began as a fun experiment in my kitchen turned into a full-blown passion. Every flavour I create is crafted with care, love, and a sprinkle of imagination. Whether it’s sweet, salty, or a bit of both, my popcorn is made to lift your mood and spark joy in your day.

    For me, popcorn is more than a snack. It’s a way of connecting with people, celebrating moments, and sharing smiles. So, whether you’re building your own bowl or grabbing your favourite single-serve, just know: I put my heart into every bite.

    It is always a delight in every bite. 💜
